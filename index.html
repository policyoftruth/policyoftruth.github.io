On Minimalism in Computing

2025.10.28 00:20:33

There's something liberating about stripping away the unnecessary. Modern web development has become bloated bits of unnecessary frameworks that assault your senses. We've forgotten that simplicity is a feature, not a limitation.

A static HTML site loads instantly. No unpacking and no node_modules directory that could collapse into a black hole. You just open your editor, write, save, and deploy. The barrier between thought and publication now collapses to zero. 

$ echo "hello world" > index.html
$ git push
# shipped.

This is how it should be. Fast. Efficient. Under your control.
